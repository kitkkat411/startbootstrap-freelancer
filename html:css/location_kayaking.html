Kayaking in San Diego Harbor 333 W Harbor Drive, San Diego, CA 92109
Description
Kayaking in San Diego Harbor is the perfect way to view the downtown skyline or watch the sunset on the water. Highlights include the beautiful scenery, gentle surf, and abundant wildlife including sea lions, dolphins, and leopard sharks. There is easy access to boat rentals, restrooms, and dining.
