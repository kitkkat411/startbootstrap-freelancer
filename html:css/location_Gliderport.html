Torrey Pines Gliderport 2800 Torrey Pines Scenic Dr., La Jolla, CA
Description
Grounded in a rich history, the Torrey Pines Gliderport is San Diego's premiere location for free flight. This includes lessons, certifications, tandem flights, equipment sales, advanced learning, and service. We serve anyone looking to extend their horizons and participate in this exciting new life style. We seek to promote all forms of free flight, and make Southern California a destination for flight enthusiasts around the world.
