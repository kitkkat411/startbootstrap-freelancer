Black's Beach Surfing 2800 Torrey Pines Scenic Dr, La Jolla, CA 92037
Description
Arguably one of San Diego’s best wave, this surf spot is great for all year round. It is known to surfers as one of the powerful surf breaks in Southern California. Black’s Beach requires a long hike down the La Jolla Cliffs and is advisable for more advanced surfers.

Reviews (2)

Fiducci B.
9/1/14

Used to be a nude beach, but forget about all that. If you want big powerful waves, go to Blacks. The best surf south of tressels. OK, maybe Wind and Sea, but you get Blacks without the attitude. Watch out though, these are some heavy waves and they can be very punishing.


James T.
9/1/14

I came here to surf with some friends and it was awesome! I live in Northern California and I wasn't sure what to expect, but the water was beautiful and warm enough for me to strip off my 3:2 wetsuit and surf in board shorts only! The waves were 4-6 feet and they were shaped perfectly! The only downside to surfing here is the horrifying hike back up the cliff when you are done.
