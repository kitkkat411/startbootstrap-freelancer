Bayshore Bikeway (Silver Strand) 1201 1st St, Coronado, CA 92118
Description
This 24 mile bike starts at the Coronado Ferry/Market Place and ends on W. 32nd street Chula Vista for a smooth and scenic ride. The Bayshore Bikeway boasts stunning views of the San Diego bay and downtown skyline. Head south along the palm-tree lined path past restaurants, cafes, and parks taking in beautiful sights such as the Coronado Bridge and Hotel del Coronado.

Reviews (2)

Jay D.
8/15/14

My friend and I started in Coronado and had no trouble following the bike path down to IB and over to CV. There were a few 'Bayshore Bikeway' signs here and there to help stay on the right path. In Chula Vista, the official bike path ends and you have to ride on city roads, but the traffic is minimal and route fairly straightforward.


Oulie T.
8/27/14

The Coronado portion is short but gorgeous. Perfect way to take in the island. Many pretty photo opportunities on the trail with gorgeous perspectives of the San Diego downtown skyline as a backdrop, or the historic Hotel Del Coronado and it's pretty beach. You could even get a photo with cool looking naval ships depending on what's in the harbor. We were impressed with the enormity of those things.
