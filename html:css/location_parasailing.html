San Diego Parasailing Adventures 844 W Mission Bay Dr, San Diego CA 92109
Description
This powerboat/gliding ride around Mission Bay is fun and exciting for all ages! San Diego Parasailing Adventures offers “Winch Direct Parasailing” which is the safest and most widely accepted form of parasailing in the world today. The entire boating adventure lasts about 1 ½ hours with each person flying in the air for about 6 to 8 minutes.

Reviews (2)

Eric H.
7/18/14

This was a great once in a lifetime experience and definitely worth it. We were a family of four, including two teenagers. The great thing about this company is that you can go up in pairs, so it is a shared experience as opposed to going up alone and not being able to share the view with someone. We were up in the air for about 8-10 minutes, and though that may seem short, after a while you have gotten the experience of being up in the air and seen the beautiful view of the ocean and San Diego.

My BF and I came here for the first time during Memorial Day and had a good time! The staff was very informative and very clear as to what to do and they made sure we enjoyed ourselves during our adventure. The only thing is being up in the air for only 6-8 minutes and paying $89.95 per person. This is quite a bit of money and we were only over the ocean for a total of 1 hour when we were told we would be there for 1.25, so I felt that our adventure was a bit rushed, but we had a good time nonetheless.
